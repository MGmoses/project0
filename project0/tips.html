Tips

Living on the deserted island comes with a lot of challenges, here will will try to give you some useful information to help you make your life easier and enjoyable.


Bells
Bells are the primary form of currency in the deserted island. You can use bells for various purposes like upgrading your house and the purchase of new items.
Tools
You will be able to craft a variety of tools using your workbench in order to help you customize your island and craft new items.
Some of these tools include: Axe, Fishing Rod, Watering Can, Net, Shovel.
DIY
You will be able to make different types of tools and furniture using DIY (Do It Yourself) recipies that you can obtain by doing different tasks in the island.

  • Axe

    Axe is the one of the first tools that you will be able to obtain in the game. You will be able to get Flimsy Axe from Tom Nook. To craft Flimsy Axe you will need 5 tree branches and 1 stone.


  • Shovel

    You will be able to obtain Flimsy Shovel from Blathers when he visits you, you can use the shovel to dig up different things such as fossils and buried bells. You will need five pieces of Hardwood in order to craft Flimsy Shovel.

  • Watering Can

    Flimsy Watering Can DIY recipie will be given to you by Tom Nook after you catch a certain number of bugs. You can use the watering can to water flowers and plants. To craft Flimsy Watering Can you will need 5 softwood.

  • Net

    You will be able to craft Flimsy Net after crafing Flimsy Fishing Rod. You can use the Flimsy Net to catch different types of bugs and insects in the island. To craft Flimsy Net you will need 5 tree branches.



  • Fishing Rod

    You can buy the Flimsy Fishing Rod from Timmy for 400 Bells. The Fishing Rod can be used to catch different kinds of fishes from the ocean and the rivers. You can craft Flimsy Fishing Rod for 5 tree branches.
