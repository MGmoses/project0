There are a number of helpful villagers who are ready to assist you on the deserted island.

Tom Nook | Timmy & Tommy | Isabelle | Blathers | The Abel Sisters | Wilbur & Orville

Tom Nook

Tom Nook is the fearless leader of Nook Enterprises who has taken the task upon himself to go with you to a deserted island. He will be helping you out with various tasks and will provide you with tools to help you make your deserted island a better place. If you need to do any construction on your island, Tom Nook can get it sorted out for a small loan of a couple thousand bells (or more). You can find Mr.Nook at the Resident Services.

Timmy & Tommy

Timmy and Tommy are Tom Nook's sons, like their father Timmy and Tommy will assist you by selling you the tools and other equipment from Resident Services tent while Tommy will provide you with useful tips outside the tent.

Isabelle

Isabelle will move into your island after you have successfully upgraded Resident Services to a building. She will help you manage the residents of the island as well as give you an island evaluation which helps you invite more people to your island.

Blathers

Blathers is the curator of your museum, he will come to your island after you have discovered a certain number of fossils. Blathers loves to talk about different types of fishes and fossils, he is not a big fan of bugs. When you have unlocked the museum he will help you keep all the creatures you discover on your island in the museum.

The Abel Sisters

The Abel Sisters are a trio of chic fashion designers. They are incharge of creating beautiful and fun designs for you to wear and express yourself. You can use the fitting room in their shop to try on different clothes and purchase them using the bells you earn.

Wilbur & Orville

Wilbur and Orville operate Dodo Airlines from where you can visit other islands to meet other people and get different materials for your island. You can also invite other players to your island via the Dodo code, or if you have a Dodo code of another player you can visit them as well.
